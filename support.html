How do I import content into ByteLecture?
You can import content in three ways: Upload PDF documents, add YouTube video URLs, or record audio directly in the app. Use the "Import New Content" button on the home screen to get started.
How do I access my summaries and flashcards?
Once your content is processed, you can access summaries, flashcards, and quizzes by tapping on any content item in your recent content list. The app will show you all available learning materials for that content.
What file types are supported for PDF uploads?
ByteLecture supports standard PDF files. Make sure your PDF is not password-protected and is under 50MB for optimal processing.
How do I upgrade to Premium?
Tap the diamond icon in the top-left corner of the home screen to access subscription options. Premium plans offer unlimited content processing and advanced features.
I'm having trouble signing in. What should I do?
Try signing out and signing back in. If you're using Google or Apple Sign-In, make sure you're signed into those accounts on your device. For persistent issues, please contact us via email.
